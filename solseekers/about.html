Who We Are

We're the Couple Who Can't Stop Going Back

It started with one trip to Cozumel. We rented gear, jumped into the water above the Palancar Reef, and within thirty seconds of descending, we both knew — this was different. That was the trip that changed everything.

Since then, we've made the journey between the US and Mexico more times than we can count. We've drifted along Cozumel's reef wall with sea turtles keeping pace. We've watched the sunrise ignite the Tulum ruins from a beach below with nobody else around. We've sailed past El Arco in Cabo with cold drinks and warm wind.

And somewhere in all of that — between the operators who changed our lives and the ones who wasted our afternoons — Sol Seekers was born.

We built this site because we kept being asked: "You guys travel there so much — what should we actually do?" Sol Seekers is our answer. Every adventure listed here is one we believe in, based on experiences we've had ourselves.

Our Mission

Skip the Guesswork. Find What's Real.

Mexico has thousands of adventures on offer. Most of them are fine. A handful are genuinely extraordinary. The problem is telling the difference before you show up.

Sol Seekers exists to close that gap. We do the legwork — we travel, we test, we talk to operators — and then we curate only the experiences worth your time and money.

We're not affiliated with resorts. We don't take kickbacks to feature low-quality tours. Every listing on this site is here because we believe it delivers.
